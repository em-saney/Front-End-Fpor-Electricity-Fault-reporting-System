About EFRS

Welcome to the Electricity Fault Reporting System, a state-of-the-art web-based application specifically designed to streamline the reporting and management of electricity faults within the hostels of Ahmadu Bello University in samaru campus, Nigeria.Our mission is to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of fault reporting, significantly reducing downtime and improving the quality of life students residing in the hostels.

Our Objectives

  • Develop a user-friendly web application that allows students to report electrical faults from their devices.
  • Create a real-time communication channel between students and the maintenance team to ensure timely responses and updates on reported faults
  • Improve the speed and accuracy of fault reporting by providing an accessible platform for students to communicate any fault issues.
  • Implement a system that allows the maintenance team to track, analyze, and manage reported faults, providing insights into recurring issues and helping to prioritize maintenance activities.
